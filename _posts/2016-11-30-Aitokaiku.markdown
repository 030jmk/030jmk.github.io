Aitokaiku uses sensors to create music that changes whether you are in a quiet space or being loud with friends, sitting at work or being chased by wolves. Aitokaiku music is different every time you turn it on because you are different.

Jarno Eerola is the co-founder and CEO of the Berlin-based startup Aitokaiku with Laurent Martin as the second co-founder and CMO.
As part of a university project team to support the startup in their social media mareting planning, we sat down with them in November 2016 to get a general idea about the mobile application that they are developing which we were later able to test as well.
