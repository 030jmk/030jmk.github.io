As part of the "Fabricate Your Living Aid and Digital Transformation of Laboratories" project (FABULANDLABS), the Wissenschaftsladen Potsdam e.V. in cooperation with the University of Potsdam and the Oberlin Werkstätten gGmbH identified use case for the production of innovative aids for the visually and physically impaired. 
Edzard, Mario, and Martin explain what the project is about from their point of view.
